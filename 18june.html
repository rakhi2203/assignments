Description

"We'll Always Have Summer," the third book in Jenny Han's "The Summer I Turned Pretty" trilogy, follows Isabel "Belly" Conklin as she navigates a complicated love triangle with the Fisher brothers, Conrad and Jeremiah. Having dated Jeremiah for two years and accepted his proposal, Belly returns to Cousins Beach to plan their wedding, but Conrad's presence forces her to confront her lingering feelings for him. The story culminates with Belly making a final decision between the brothers, realizing she must break one of their hearts.
